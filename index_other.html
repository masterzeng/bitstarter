Education is a basic human right that helps an idividual's physical, intellectual, and moral development with the goal of freeing themselves from the limiting implications of poverty, ignorance and other socioeconomic circumstances.

It also drives an individual to develop a sense of Patriotism and Nationalism which are qualities that help shape our nation's future leaders.

Our government provides free basic education and even subsidizes college education. But even free or subsidized education is unsustainable for most Filipino.

Statistics show that out of 10 students who enter grade 1: 7 will finish elementary, 3 will finish highschool, and only 1 will finish college.

A major reason is poverty.

In hindsight, most parents have their hands full in providing their children with food, clothing, and shelter than worry about school expenses.

Singkolar aims to address that problem by imploring on everyone's inherent generosity and provide funding to help these kids reach their full potential.

FAQ

What is Singkolar?
Singkolar is a crowdfunding platform where everyone can make a difference in the lives of our youth and ensure their education with as little as 5 pesos.
Why build this?
Singkolar draws inspiration from crowdfunding projects such as gittip and kickstarter.
We believe that the generosity that exudes in these projects can help solve our prevailing problems in our education system.
What do you need to get this off the ground?
At the moment all I need is you support and interest with the project to help me determine actual interest for such a solution.
